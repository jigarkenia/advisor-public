PAN India Professional Tax Rates (2026)

A comprehensive guide to state-wise PT slabs, PTRC/PTEC registration, and compliance due dates for employers and professionals across India.

What is Professional Tax?

It is a tax levied by State Governments on salaried employees and professionals (CAs, Lawyers, Doctors, Directors). It is capped at a maximum of ₹2,500 per year and is deductible under the Income-tax Act, 1961.

When is it Applicable?

  • Registration is mandatory within 30 days of employing staff.
  • No minimum staff requirement; it applies when the first staff member joins.
  • Opening a new branch in a PT-applicable state requires fresh registration within 30 days.

Who is Exempted?

  • Parents of children with permanent/mental disabilities.
  • Individuals with permanent physical disabilities (including blindness).
  • Senior citizens above 65 years of age.
  • Armed Forces members (Army, Air Force, Navy).

Understanding PTRC vs PTEC

PTRC

Full Form Professional Tax Registration Certificate
Purpose Obtained by an employer to deduct and deposit professional tax from employees’ salaries.
Filing Requires monthly or half-yearly payments and formal return filings as per state laws.

PTEC

Full Form Professional Tax Enrolment Certificate
Purpose Obtained by an employer or independent practitioner (CA, Doctor, Director) to pay their own professional tax.
Filing Usually requires a simple annual payment. No complex return filing is required.
